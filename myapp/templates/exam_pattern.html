Exam Pattern

Total Duration: 60 minutes

Total Questions: 60 questions (10 per section)

The Child Aptitude Assessment consists of 6 sections designed to evaluate different aspects of a child's abilities and interests:

Section 1: Logical Reasoning

Tests the ability to identify patterns, logical rules, and sequences. Includes puzzles and deduction problems.

Section 2: Numerical Ability

Evaluates mathematical skills, number sense, and problem-solving using numbers.

Section 3: Verbal Reasoning

Assesses language skills, vocabulary, comprehension, and verbal problem-solving.

Section 4: Data Intrepretation

Measures the ability to mentally manipulate shapes, visualize objects from different angles, and understand spatial relationships.

Section 5: Numerical Ability-1

Evaluates mathematical skills, number sense, and problem-solving using numbers.

Section 6: Reasoning Ability

Explores creative thinking, imagination, and areas of natural interest through open-ended questions.
